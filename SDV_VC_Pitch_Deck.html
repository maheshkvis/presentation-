India — Regulatory Tailwind

DPDPA 2023 Creates the Legal Framework
We Need to Operate

Section 14 — Right to Nominate

Allows data principals to appoint nominees to manage their digital data after death or incapacity. This is exactly Vaultomi's core mechanism.

Rule 13 (DPDP Rules 2025)

Operationalizes the nomination process. Notified Nov 13, 2025. Phased implementation underway. We're building on law, not around it.

RBI Nominee Update (Nov 2025)

Banks now allow up to 4 nominees per deposit account — simultaneous or successive. Signals regulatory momentum.

Additional Compliance Framework

  • IT Act, 2000 — Electronic records, digital signatures
  • Indian Succession Act, 1925 — Property succession (lex situs)
  • RBI Guidelines — Data localization, KYC/AML
  • IRDAI Guidelines — Insurance claim & nominee processes
  • Bharatiya Sakshya Adhiniyam — Electronic records as evidence
Legal Feasibility: HIGH

DPDPA Section 14 + Rule 13 provide explicit legal backing. Supreme Court (Shakti Yezdani, 2023) clarified nominee framework. Our workflow is legally sound.
